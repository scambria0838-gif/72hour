4. SMS Communication Policy

4.1 Consent

We only send SMS messages to customers who have explicitly consented through:

  • Verbal consent during sales calls (documented in our CRM)
  • Written consent via checkbox on our website forms

4.2 Message Types and Frequency

  • During Setup (72 hours): 3-5 messages with critical updates
  • After Launch: 1-2 messages per month for account updates
  • Promotional (Optional): Maximum 2 messages per month

You can opt-out of SMS communications at any time by:

Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan. We are not responsible for carrier charges.
